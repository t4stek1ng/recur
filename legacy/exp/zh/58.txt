There is a multiple-choice question: Sound waves moving at 350 m/s diffract out of a speaker enclosure with an opening that is a long rectangular slit 0.14 m across. At about what frequency will the sound first disappear at an angle of 45q from the normal to the speaker face?. The options are: A. 500 Hz, B. 1,750 Hz, C. 2,750 Hz, D. 3,500 Hz. Why the correct answer is: C. 2,750 Hz?
