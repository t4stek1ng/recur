There is a multiple-choice question: At 20°C, a pipe open at both ends resonates at a frequency of 440 hertz. At what frequency does the same pipe resonate on a particularly cold day when the speed of sound is 3 percent lower than it would be at 20°C ?. The options are: A. 414 Hz, B. 427 Hz, C. 433 Hz, D. 440 Hz. Why the correct answer is: A. 414 Hz?
