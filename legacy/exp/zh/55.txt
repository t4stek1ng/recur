There is a multiple-choice question: A photon strikes an electron of mass m that is initially at rest, creating an electron-positron pair. The photon is destroyed and the positron and two electrons move off at equal speeds along the initial direction of the photon. The energy of the photon was. The options are: A. mc^2, B. 2mc^2, C. 3mc^2, D. 4mc^2. Why the correct answer is: B. 2mc^2?
