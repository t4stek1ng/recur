There is a multiple-choice question: Protons used in cancer therapy are typically accelerated to about 0.6c . How much work must be done on a particle of mass m in order for it to reach this speed, assuming it starts at rest?. The options are: A. 0.25mc^2, B. 0.60mc^2, C. 0.67mc^2, D. 1.25mc^2. Why the correct answer is: B. 0.60mc^2?
