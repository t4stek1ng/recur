There is a multiple-choice question: An astronomer observes a very small moon orbiting a planet and measures the moon’s minimum and maximum distances from the planet’s center and the moon’s maximum orbital speed. Which of the following CANNOT be calculated from these measurements?. The options are: A. Mass of the moon, B. Mass of the planet, C. Minimum speed of the moon, D. Period of the orbit. Why the correct answer is: C. Minimum speed of the moon?
