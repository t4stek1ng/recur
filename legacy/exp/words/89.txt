There is a multiple-choice question: Which of the following describes the fallacy of appeal to tradition?. The options are: A. reasoning that a claim must be true because it's been made so often, B. reasoning that a claim must be true because a regular person believes it, C. reasoning that a claim must be true because it's been believed in the past, D. reasoning that a claim must be true because a lot of people believe it. Why the correct answer is: A. reasoning that a claim must be true because it's been made so often?
