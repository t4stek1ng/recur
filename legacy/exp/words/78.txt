There is a multiple-choice question: A white noise process will have

(i) A zero mean

(ii) A constant variance

(iii) Autocovariances that are constant

(iv) Autocovariances that are zero except at lag zero. The options are: A. (ii) and (iv) only, B. (i) and (iii) only, C. (i), (ii), and (iii) only, D. (i), (ii), (iii), and (iv). Why the correct answer is: D. (i), (ii), (iii), and (iv)?
